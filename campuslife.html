CAMPUS LIFE AND STORIES

Rhyzza Arioste's STE Life

As an STE student, my campus life has been a mix of highs and lows, much like the turning of a wheel. During the times I’ve been "above the wheel," I’ve had opportunities to join competitions such as science fairs and school press conferences, which have allowed me to grow, learn, and meet new people.

However, there are also moments when I’ve been "below the wheel." These moments include sacrifices like sleepless nights and skipped meals to keep up with the demands of excelling in my studies and extracurricular activities.

Despite the challenges, my campus life has been fulfilling, thanks to my supportive friends who stand by me through every up and down. My classmates, though we sometimes have misunderstandings, have also played a big role in making my campus life safer, more enjoyable, and fun. Lastly, the teachers I’ve encountered have been instrumental in guiding and inspiring me to reach my goals.

Overall, my campus life as an STE student is both challenging and rewarding, filled with meaningful experiences and valuable connections.
